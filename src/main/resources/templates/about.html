    What We Do

    The "Trip to Kyrgyzstan" website was created by the Tourist Information Center with the aim of promoting the tourism potential of Kyrgyzstan. Here, you can find all the information about various travel destinations and opportunities for tourists planning to visit Kyrgyzstan. Opportunities for Travelers Kyrgyzstan is a land of heavenly mountains and indescribable nature, the cradle of nomadic culture and the land of the epic hero Manas. It is a place where travelers will always find many interesting and extraordinary discoveries! On the website, we have gathered the most interesting and useful information about rest and travel in Kyrgyzstan. You have the chance to discover something new about the country, explore the most popular travel destinations, learn about upcoming events, book accommodations, and choose the right tour. For more information, please contact our information center and send us your requests. We, together with our partners, will select a program tailored just for you! Opportunities for Tourism Business Representatives As part of the "Trip to Kyrgyzstan" project, we are creating a database of tourism industry representatives in Kyrgyzstan. If your business is related to recreation, tourism, and travel in any way, you can contact us and register your profile on the website. This will provide you with a unique opportunity to promote your offerings, and tourists from all around the world will be able to learn about your services and contact you via our website, call center, or directly.
