    • Naryn: Best Attractions

    Kel-Suu Lake

    On the way to the lake, there is a stunning view of the At-Bashi mountain range, the Eki-Naryn Canyon (where the Small Naryn and Naryn rivers converge), and the 8th-century Koshoi-Korgon fortress.

    Kel-Suu Lake

    The magnificent Kel-Suu Lake was formed thousands of years ago due to the destruction of a mountain range. It is located in the Kok-Kiya Valley (3,520 m) in the Naryn region, near the border with China.
