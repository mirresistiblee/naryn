Naryn, Land of Majestic Peaks and Valleys

With its diverse landscapes, the Naryn region is home to countless breathtaking and memorable mountain destinations, perfect for visiting year-round, whether in summer or winter. From its vast high-altitude plateaus to pristine forests, the Naryn region offers visitors stunning lakes, charming villages, and historical settlements rich in traditions, culture, and delightful local cuisine.

The Exchanges

In the Naryn region of Kyrgyzstan, various events are held to promote tourism. These include traditional cultural festivals, such as ethno-festivals, as well as eco-tours and eco-festivals that attract tourists interested in the region's natural heritage. Naryn also hosts sporting events like kok-boru tournaments and tourism exhibitions, such as "Kyrgyzstan. Tourism and Leisure," where tourism development is discussed. These events help promote Naryn as an attractive tourist destination.
